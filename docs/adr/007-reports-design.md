# ADR-007: Reports design

Status: Accepted

## Decision

Generate organization-owned, versioned report-card snapshots from finalized factual assessment results and finalized attendance within explicit periods. Preserve published/withdrawn snapshots, whitelist template options, and provide formula-safe CSV plus escaped PDF output.

## Consequences

Historical output is reproducible and lifecycle-controlled. Current placement limits historical accuracy until enrolment history exists; Reports does not make promotion, ranking, AI, or portal decisions.
