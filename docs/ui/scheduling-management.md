# Scheduling management

`/scheduling` is the factual organization dashboard. `/scheduling/timetable` provides bounded daily/weekly navigation and scoped filters. Room and template directories are under `/scheduling/rooms` and `/scheduling/templates`; calendar and period links reuse Academics.

Templates validate before activation and materialize only explicit ranges. Closures and conflicts are counted and skipped. Rescheduling preserves the original and creates a linked replacement. Cancellation, completion, missed state, assignments, attendance links, and history remain auditable.

Optimization/AI generation, exam timetables, notifications, external sync, conferencing provisioning, public sharing, booking, portals, and mobile apps are excluded.
